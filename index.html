DRINK UP!

What is this...

The objective is simple:

Drink every single shade of every single colour imaginable. (These constitute your unique drinks.)

How though?

To fill up your cup, select a colour using the colour selector in the top-left corner and then click on the glass (when it's empty).

And when it's full, you may click again to drink your brew.

Get to it. Drink Up!
